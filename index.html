About Me

Engineering Philosophy & Approach

I approach engineering challenges with a systems-thinking mindset, emphasizing rigorous design justification, iterative prototyping, and validation through testing. My academic and personal projects follow a structured engineering design process from problem definition and background research to detailed design, fabrication, and critical reflection.

My work integrates mechanical design, embedded systems, and controls to create innovative robotic and mechatronic solutions. I am particularly interested in autonomous systems, robotic manipulation, and the intersection of advanced manufacturing with functional prototype development.

I am actively seeking research or industry opportunities where I can contribute to cutting-edge projects in robotics, autonomous vehicles, or precision mechatronic systems.
