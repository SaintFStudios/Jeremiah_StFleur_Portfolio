Engineering Design Process

My academic and project work follows a rigorous, documented engineering methodology. Each portfolio entry is structured to demonstrate competency in each phase.

1. Problem Definition

Clearly articulate the problem, constraints (cost, size, materials), and success criteria. Document the initial assignment or project goal.

2. Background Research

Investigate existing solutions, academic papers, and technical resources. Cite sources and analyze prior art to inform the design approach.

3. Brainstorming & Concept

Generate a minimum of 3 distinct solution concepts. Develop sketches and CAD models to explore different approaches before down-selection.

4. Detailed Design

Create complete CAD models, engineering drawings, and performance simulations. Justify material, component, and tolerance selections.

5. Fabrication & Assembly

Document the build process with a dated work log (updated every ~1.5 hrs). Note challenges, iterations, and solutions during manufacturing.

6. Test, Analyze, Reflect

Perform quantitative and qualitative testing. Analyze results against criteria. Document failures, lessons learned, and proposed design improvements.
