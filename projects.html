S.C.A.N. Drone (Servo-Controlled App Navigator)

Personal Project | Mar 2025 - Present

A custom-designed terrestrial drone using continuous-rotation servos for differential steering, controlled via a mobile application with multiple operational modes.

RoboticsESP32PID ControlMobile UI
  • Problem Statement: Create a compact, precise, wirelessly controllable mobile platform for modular payload testing.
  • Design & Implementation: Engineered chassis with high-grip TPU wheels; implemented positional feedback for precise steering; developed RemoteXY mobile app.
  • Integration & Results: Consolidated sensor and audio systems on a single solderless PCB, reducing footprint by ~20%.

Compressed Air Engine & Powered Slicer

Course Project (EML2322L) | Jan 2025 - May 2025

A complete design-manufacture-assemble-test cycle project involving a machined air engine and an engine-powered slicing mechanism.

DFMCNC/LatheTolerance AnalysisMechanism Design
  • Constraints: 4-12 parts, ≤1 CNC operation, ≤$75 budget.
  • Engine: Machined 3 interchangeable builds on lathe/mill/CNC, holding "thou-level" fits.
  • Slicer: Designed rocker four-bar mechanism (~3.5" stroke) with UHMW slider and PETG chassis.
  • Documentation: Issued formal DFM updates, ECNs, MIRs; created costed BOM; used OTS brackets to reduce machining time 30%.
